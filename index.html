Bio

Hi there, I’m Michaela.

I am a hard working, driven person with a day-dreaming spirit. I find that I am a mixture of analytical & creative, often seeking a balance between the mind & matters of the heart. Because of this, I have been working towards a certification at University of Texas in UX/UI Design. This opportunity has allowed me to better empathize with the problems of others, all the while finding a niche for creative expression & detail-oriented research to bring ideas to life & solve the problems people may face in their user experience.
